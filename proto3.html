 portfolio mix
The optimal mix balances cost and reliability across the full demand volume. A single cheap supplier looks efficient — until they miss a shipment. Spreading volume across 2–3 reliable suppliers costs a bit more but dramatically reduces stockout risk. Most sourcing teams cap at 3–4 suppliers per category to keep relationships manageable.
Takeaway → Sourcing isn't just about the cheapest price — it's portfolio construction. Cost, reliability, capacity, and lead time all interact. The right mix depends on your risk tolerance and what a stockout actually costs the business.
